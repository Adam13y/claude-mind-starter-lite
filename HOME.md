## How this vault works

Claude reads `CLAUDE.md` at the start of a session and follows the protocol in it: read the task files, tell you what's active, write down anything new as it comes up, and close out the session when you say **"update your mind."**

You don't have to manage the vault. You have to *use* it, and let Claude keep it.

## Notes

Everything else lives in `Notes/`, linked with `[[wikilinks]]`. There's no required structure — the task files are the spine; notes are whatever you need them to be.
